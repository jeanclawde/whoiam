# Moodboard notes — remicorp

## Strongest axis

The center should be **annual-report authority + standards-manual grammar**. IBM annual reports are the priority reference: abstract, serious, confident, memorable without shouting.

## Useful tensions

1. **IBM / Westinghouse / Mobil** — corporate restraint, one memorable move, abstract symbol logic.
2. **Olivetti / CCA** — warmth, culture, art, wit, human intelligence.
3. **NASA / EPA / NYCTA** — instructions, systems, specifications, institutional usefulness.
4. **Order / Standards Manual** — how to translate the above into 2026 without making a costume.
5. **Brutalist architecture** — use only as accent: mass, grid, civic confidence, concrete texture.

## Font direction

Historical references: Helvetica / Neue Haas Grotesk, Univers, Akzidenz-Grotesk, Trade Gothic / Franklin Gothic.

Modern practical baseline: **IBM Plex Sans + IBM Plex Mono**. Later test Inter, Suisse Int’l, Neue Haas Grotesk Text, Söhne, or licensed Univers/Helvetica depending budget.

## Color direction

Base: warm off-white, black/ink, neutral grey. Add one institutional signal color and one human accent.

Candidate palettes:
- IBM/Mobil axis: blue + red on white/black.
- Lufthansa axis: navy + yellow, more transport/system than tech.
- Olivetti/CCA axis: red/orange + teal + warm paper.
- EPA axis: green/blue, but risk of environmental association.

## Avoid

SaaS gradients, glassmorphism, AI blobs, pure nostalgia, fake paper aging, brutalism overpowering the graphic identity, too many colors.
