00 / Synthesis

The useful tension.

remicorp should borrow operating principles, not surfaces: the confidence of annual reports, the grammar of standards manuals, the warmth of humanist corporate design, and the restraint of contemporary studios that know when to shut up.

Annual-report authority

Investor-grade seriousness, abstract covers, tables, maps, operations photography, sober captions.

Standards as theatre

Manual pages, misuse diagrams, measurements, signage families — documentation as desirable object.

One memorable deviation

Mobil's red O, CN's line, NASA's worm: a constrained move repeated until it becomes inevitable.

2026, not costume

Use modern tooling, web motion, variable systems and clean accessibility — no fake paper aging.
